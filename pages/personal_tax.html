AYAPS offers a comprehensive personal tax service where all your financial needs can be deal with under one roof. Our aim is to ensure that the burden placed on clients by tax compliance matters is minimised, while at the same time they benefit from all relevant allowances and relieves to ensure that tax liabilities are mitigated wherever possible. Our personal approach means that you develop a one to one relations ship with your tax advisor, thus enabling us to identify opportunities for appropriate and relevant tax planning, while at the same time giving you a forum to discuss a particular issues with some one fully acquainted with your affairs.

Our personal tax return service is designed to save you time and give you peace of mind. We will prepare your tax return, tell you what to pay and when to pay it and deal with all matters generally relating to self assessment.

The continue changing in the capital gain tax even more complex than ever. The rules and the calculations are complex and great care and planning is needed to ensure that the maximum benefit of tax payer relief is achieved particularly where business has been sold. It is now absolutely vital that proper tax advice is sought before disposal of assets take place if the capital gain tax liability is to be minimized.

There are a significant number of tax allowances schemes available within the Nigerian tax system, and our team of tax specialist liaise heavily with our dedicated financial services professionals to ensure that you have access to a wide range of specialist advice in all aspects of your personal financial planning needs, ranging from inheritance and estate planning through to pensions and investment advice. We can also assist you with planning for international assignments or coming to Nigeria from abroad on double taxation relief.
