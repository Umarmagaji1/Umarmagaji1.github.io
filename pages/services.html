    Outsource Internal Audit

    The traditional model of the internal audit function was as a department internal to the host organization with a reporting line to either the CEO or the CFO and the Audit Committee. Much has changed in the discipline of internal audit over the past 10 years. The change has come about due to the increasing pressure on Audit Committees to take on expanded roles to ensure the effectiveness of governance and the work of the oversight body for financial reporting, risk management and the control framework of organizations.
